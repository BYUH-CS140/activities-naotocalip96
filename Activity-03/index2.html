Main Heroes and Supporting Characters

Peter Parker / Spider-Man

Peter Parker is a high school student turned superhero after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He juggles personal struggles, school life, and superhero responsibilities while fighting crime as Spider-Man.

Mary Jane Watson

Mary Jane is Peter’s long-time love interest. She's confident, kind, and often serves as an emotional anchor for Peter.

Aunt May

Aunt May raised Peter after the loss of his parents. She is a moral guide in his life and deeply cares for his well-being, often unaware of his life as Spider-Man.

Uncle Ben

Uncle Ben’s death was a turning point in Peter's life. His famous advice, "With great power comes great responsibility," shapes Spider-Man’s mission.

Harry Osborn

Harry Osborn is Peter Parker's best friend and the son of Norman Osborn (the Green Goblin). Though loyal, Harry often struggles with his father's legacy and his complicated relationship with Spider-Man, leading to moments of both friendship and conflict.
