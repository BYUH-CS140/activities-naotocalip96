Mt.Fuji

Mount Fuji is the tallest mountain in Japan, standing at 3,776 meters (12,389 feet). It is an iconic symbol of Japan, known for its perfectly symmetrical cone shape and cultural significance.

Located on Honshu Island, Mount Fuji is a popular destination for tourists, hikers, and photographers, especially during the climbing season in summer.

It has been a sacred site in Japanese religion for centuries and is often featured in traditional art and literature.

On clear days, it can be seen from Tokyo, offering a breathtaking view of Japan's natural beauty.
