Lake Biwa

Lake Biwa is the largest freshwater lake in Japan, located in Shiga Prefecture, near Kyoto. It is one of the oldest lakes in the world, believed to be over 4 million years old.

Surrounded by mountains and historic towns, Lake Biwa is known for its natural beauty, diverse wildlife, and cultural significance.

The lake provides water for millions of people and is a popular spot for activities like boating, fishing, and cycling.

Many visitors also enjoy visiting temples nearby, such as Enryaku-ji on Mount Hiei.
